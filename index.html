Our Story

Welcome to In a Pita! Founded by Jonathan Stein, our journey began in Santa Cruz County, inspired by a lifelong passion for cooking ignited in his mother's kitchen. Jonathan honed his culinary expertise at the prestigious California Culinary Academy, Le Cordon Bleu, specializing in the vibrant and authentic flavors of Middle Eastern cuisine.

We use the freshest farm-to-table ingredients to craft dishes that are delicious and visually stunning. We proudly offer a wide range of vegan and vegetarian options.
